Ms. Chen Xueyun (b. 1990) is a founder and senior executive with a dual background in industrial operations and professional investment, with over a decade of management experience across IC manufacturing, new energy, the internet, private equity and securities investment. She currently serves as Senior Vice President at a leading 12-inch wafer fab (55nm and more advanced nodes), overseeing investment & financing, development planning, supply chain and legal affairs; previously head of a top-tier investment institution and Executive Vice President of a listed company. With strengths in strategy, investment & financing, operations and capacity building, she applies a forward-looking industrial-investment approach across the full industrial life cycle.
